One particular book which is recommended reding is The Street Lawyer by John Grisham . this book is about a lawyer who begin re-evaluating his priorities in life when a bad incident occurs within his law firm .Consequently, he becomes acquainted with the inner city streets, and realizes the harsh existence of the homeless, and vows to give them a chance in the courts. The Street Lawyer is a great book. It is well written an interesting. Other books by John Grisham include The Firm, The Pelican Brief, and The Client
